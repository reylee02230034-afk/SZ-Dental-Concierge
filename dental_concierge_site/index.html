Frequently Asked Questions

Do the dentists speak English?

Yes, Dr. Li and our lead medical staff are fluent in English. You will have a dedicated English-speaking concierge with you throughout your visit.

Do I need a visa for China?

China now implements a 240-hour (10-day) visa-free transit policy for citizens of 55 countries (including US, UK, Canada, Australia, etc.) when traveling to a third country. Additionally, citizens of several countries (e.g., Singapore, Thailand, Malaysia, and many European nations) enjoy 15 to 30 days of visa-free entry without transit requirements. We can advise on the latest regulations for your nationality.

How long do I need to stay?

For implants, the first visit usually requires 3-5 days. A return visit for the final crown placement happens 3-6 months later and takes 3-5 days. Immediate load options (Teeth in a Day) are available for suitable candidates.

Is it safe?

Absolutely. We adhere to strict hospital-grade sterilization protocols. We use single-use instruments wherever possible and autoclave sterilization for all surgical tools.
